About Us

Find a Cat

Every day, our no-kill shelter receives cats that have run out of time at high kill facilities due to lack of space and we find them a forever home. Browse for your next kitty below!

All animals are spayed/neutered, microchipped, given all vaccines and boosters that are required. They are dewormed, FeLV/FIV tested and on and frontline during their stay at AH.

Find a Dog

Every day, our no-kill shelter receives dogs that have run out of time at high kill facilities due to lack of space and we find them a forever home. Browse for your next puppy below!

All animals are spayed/neutered, microchipped, given all vaccines and boosters that are required. They are dewormed, heartworm tested and on heartgard and frontline during their stay at AH.

Adopt a pet

When a pet is adopted it allows us to save another life.
Like people, animals have distinct personalities that make them unique. At Animal Heaven, we are proud to say that the dogs and cats are treated like our own. We care enough to be certain they are treated with the comfort and respect that they deserve and we make every effort to ensure that they go to the right, forever, permanent, loving home.

Foster a pet

Short-or-long-term foster care is great for folks who cannot adopt.
Foster care is essential to our mission at Animal Heaven. Foster families enable us to rescue more dogs and cats from high kill facilities. We could not save all of these lives without you!
